ü§î Basic View

Monday Left Me Broken Cat a.k.a Mr. Peebles is a meme that started spreading around TikTok in March of 2023 which features a singing cat.
More exactly, a cat with a human-like face which was animated with an app called Revive, making it sing "Waiting For Love" by Avicii, a late EDM artist.
These also featured "edits" of the cat with various effects and templates from CapCut.

‚ùî Origin

First posted by user @beast_warrior on TikTok, which is known to be the earliest post about this meme. The video got 410k+ views in just a month!

üåê Spread

@beast_warrior continued to upload simillar videos of the cat.
New TikTokers started to hop on the trend.

On Feb 27th of 2023, a Tiktok account called '@monday_left_me_broken' also posted the same video garnering over
5.9 million views, just also within a month's time!

On March 3rd, another user called '@dehren2010' posted a video where the cat is revealed by cracking open a Watermelon which was edited using a CapCut template.
It garnered around 696k views in just two weeks!
