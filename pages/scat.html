😺 Smurf Cat

✍️ Author: AlluXD

⏱️ 15 min read (estimated)


🤔 Basic View

The Smurf Cat meme has taken the internet by storm. It features a Smurf with a cat-like face
This meme started on Instagram and Tiktok. People even made different variations which
we will discuss later on!


❔ Origin

The Smurf cat meme was first posted on TikTok with the Russian hashtag #aлуа, and
is also known among Russians as "шайлушай" (English Translation: Shailushai)
It's creator is Nate Hallinan, it was created in 2014, and can be found on his website: Click here!

🌐 Spread

The meme started spreading a lot around Instagram and TikTok before it came to YouTube.
It features a Smurf with a cat face in a forest-type of background.
The meme also has the song 'The Spectre' by Alan Walker playing in the background.
